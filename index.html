    Who We Are

    The Vertex Society (TVS) is a non-political platform that brings together students, alumni, mentors and professionals to learn, lead, innovate and create positive impact.

    Vision

    To become a nationally respected and globally connected platform developing ethical leaders and innovators.

    Mission

    Creating opportunities for leadership, learning, innovation and community service.

    Impact

    Empowering students through projects, research, events and social initiatives.
